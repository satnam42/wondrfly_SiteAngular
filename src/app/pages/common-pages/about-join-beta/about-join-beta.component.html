We’re in Beta. What does that mean?

It means that full access to Wondrfly is restricted to members of our beta community only. But never fear! You too can join the Wondrfly Beta community.

All you have to do is share our passion for seeing kids learn and grow through educational, engaging activities catered to their individual needs.

Beta Users Receive Huge Benefits!

  • Unlimited and unrestricted access to over thousands of kids’ activity programs in Jersey City.
  • Early access to all Wondrfly’s features and the ability to provide feedback and comments
  • Exclusive parenting content and resources
  • Program recommendations, personalized for your child
  • 2 referrals to invite friends and family to join the beta program
