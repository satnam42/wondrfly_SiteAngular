Why Become a Beta User?


To see all Wondrfly has to offer, Join the Beta!

Beta Users gain early access to all Wondrfly’s features and a unique voice in the evolution of Wondrfly.

If you’re constantly curious and searching for the best kids’ activities in Jersey City and Hoboken, then you’re a perfect candidate.

Beta Users Receive Unique Benefits.

    As a Beta User, you’ll be the first to experience all of Wondrfly’s features as they are released.

    You’ll also have the opportunity to give feedback on everything, meaning you’ll have a one-of-a-kind voice in shaping the Wondrfly experience.

    You even have the exclusive power to grow the Beta program by inviting friends and family!

    More perks of becoming a Beta User include:
  • Unlimited kids’ activity searches.
  • Exclusive content and downloadable resources.
  • Personalized activity recommendations
